Empowering the Digital Age

The CIT (Computer Information Technology) program is designed to produce competent technical professionals who can meet the demands of the rapidly evolving IT industry.

Our 3-year DAE program covers a broad spectrum of computing disciplines, from fundamental programming to advanced networking and web development. Students gain hands-on experience in our fully equipped computer labs, ensuring they are ready to tackle real-world challenges.
