Powering the Nation

Electrical Technology is the cornerstone of modern industry. Our DAE program provides deep insights into power generation, transmission, and distribution systems.

Students work with high-tech equipment in our labs to understand circuit theory, motor controls, and industrial wiring, preparing them for critical roles in the energy sector.
