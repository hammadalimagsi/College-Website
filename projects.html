Pharmacy Management System

This project automates the inventory and sales process of a pharmacy. It keeps track of medicine stock, expiry dates, and supplier information. Features include:

  • Real-time stock monitoring.
  • Automated alerts for low stock.
  • Sales reporting and billing generation.

Department: CIT

Bridge Protection System

An automated system designed to monitor vehicle weight before entering a bridge. If the weight exceeds the safe limit, barriers automatically close to prevent entry.

Department: Electrical
