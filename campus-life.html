Life at GCT

A vibrant community of learners, innovators, and leaders.

Beyond Books

Holistic Development

At GCT Bhakkar, education goes beyond the classroom. We provide countless opportunities for students to explore their passions, develop leadership skills, and create lifelong memories.

Student Activities

Annual Sports Gala

Our highly anticipated sports week brings together students from all departments to compete in cricket, football, athletics, and more. It helps foster team spirit and physical well-being.

Seminars & Workshops

We regularly invite industry experts and guest speakers to conduct workshops on emerging technologies, career guidance, and soft skills development.

Project Competitions

Final-year students showcase their innovative projects in the Annual Tech Expo. This platform connects students with potential employers and industry leaders.

Student Societies

From the Debating Society to the Robotics Club, our diverse student organizations offer a platform for every interest, helping you hone your talents.

Social Responsibility

Community Engagement

We believe in giving back. Our students actively participate in community service programs, including blood donation drives, tree plantation campaigns, and technical skills workshops for the underprivileged. These activities instill a sense of civic responsibility and empathy.
