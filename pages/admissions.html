Join a Legacy of Technical Excellence

Government College of Technology Bhakkar invites applications for its 3-Year Diploma of Associate Engineer (DAE) programs. We are committed to providing high-quality technical education that empowers students to excel in the global industry. Our admissions are strictly on a merit basis, ensuring a competitive and intellectual environment.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Matriculation (Science): Candidate must have passed Matric with Science (Physics, Chemistry, Math) or equivalent.
  • TSP / Vocational: Candidates with TSP/Vocational certification in relevant fields are also eligible.
  • Age Limit: Maximum age limit for morning shift is 18 years (relaxable for special cases). No age limit for the evening shift.
  • Fitness: Candidate must be medically fit for technical training.

Required Documents

  • Original Matric Certificate & 3 attested photocopies.
  • Character Certificate from the last attended institution.
  • Domicile Certificate (Attested Copy).
  • CNIC / B-Form of the student and Father/Guardian.
  • 6 Recent Passport Size Photographs (Blue background).
